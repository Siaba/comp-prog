About Codechamps++

Codechamps++ is the brainchild of Griffin Kepp, Martin Lavendier, Casey Reagan, and Nick Hain. We wanted to change the way that people compete and give them a thirst to learn more code. Pushing people to new limits with what they can do with coding is what we wish to do with Codechamps++. Competition encourages one to be better in an effort to keep up with an ever-advancing world.
